# Privacy Policy Last updated: 2026-02-24 This Privacy Policy describes how Mapstrix: Junior Map Quest ("the App") handles information. ## 1. Data We Collect The App does not require account sign-in and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number. The App stores gameplay and settings locally on the device (for example, progress, options, and audio preferences) using local app storage. ## 2. Data Shared With Third Parties The App may request publicly available media/content from third-party services at runtime. These requests are made by the device directly to those services: - Wikipedia APIs (landmark summaries/images) - FlagCDN (country flag images) - Google Fonts (font assets loaded via stylesheet import) When requests are made, those providers may receive technical request data such as IP address, user-agent, and request metadata according to their own policies. ## 3. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking The App currently does not include: - third-party analytics SDKs - advertising SDKs - crash-reporting SDKs If this changes in a future release, this policy will be updated before release. ## 4. Audio and Device Features The App uses text-to-speech functionality to provide narration on supported devices. ## 5. Children's Privacy The App is designed as a kid-friendly educational game. The App does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information from children. ## 6. Data Retention Locally stored gameplay/settings data remains on the device until the app data is cleared or the app is uninstalled. ## 7. Security No method of transmission or storage is guaranteed 100% secure. The App uses standard platform/browser mechanisms and does not operate a custom backend for player accounts. ## 8. Your Choices - You can clear app data in device settings. - You can uninstall the app at any time. ## 9. Changes to This Policy This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date above. ## 10.
